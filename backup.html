Painting app

PaintMyMuseic is an exciting web app that blends the joy of painting with the magic of music. Our motivation behind this project was to explore the fusion of colors and melodies, creating a unique artistic experience through the lens of web development. While painting with each color, a song will play specific to that color! Throughout our journey, we encountered challenges that helped us grow. One valuable lesson we learned was the importance of optimizing performance to ensure smooth interaction between painting and music. By improving our code and using efficient algorithms, we enhanced the overall user experience. To conclude, we are thrilled with how PaintByMuseic turned out and we invite you to experience it for yourself.

Technologies used: React, Typescript, HTML, CSS
