Cultural Blog - Emotional Intelligence

What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional intelligence is the ability to mange your own emotions and understand others’ emotions, and use that information to help make your decisions.

How is EQ different to IQ?

IQ measures your logical thinking and problem solving. EQ measures your ability to recognize emotion in yourself and others, and how well you use that ability to guide your decisions.

Why is EQ important?

EQ is important because it helps you build relationships and reduce stress in yourself and others interacting with you. Because of this, it is essential to being collaborative with people you interact with every day (family, workmates, etc).
